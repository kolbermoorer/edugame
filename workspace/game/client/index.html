Welcome to my game!
This instruction should help you to open and play your first game.

Click on 'Next' to proceed.

Game Mode Decide if you want to play alone, with your teammate or against him.
Points Based on the game mode you get different points for a correct or false answer.
Game Ends A game ends if all cards are played. You get points per question and not per game.
Category A category is the umbrella term of multiple topics with the same scope.
Topic A topic is the theme that have all cards in common. Each game has 3 topics, you choose 1.
Cards Maximum number of cards that can be chosen from. 8 cards per topic represents one stack.
Level Shows the awareness of the cards. Higher levels might be more difficult.
Dynamic The cards and levels change through game. A maximum of 30 topics can be played.
Warm Up Phase You have 5 minutes to gain as much as possible knowledge about the cards in the game.
Card Stack Each of the stacks contains 8 cards. If you click on one card, a Wikipedia page is opened on the left.
Wikipedia Everything that is inside the drawn red box may be important for the game. Remember as much as possible!
Start If you want to start the game before the 5 minutes are over, click on the start button.
Create Question Click on one of the cards at the top to create a question. The card will be opened afterwards.
Answer Click on one of the answer boxes in the middle. If the question makes totally no sense, click on red box.
Feedback After you have answered the question, you are asked to rate the quality of the question once per property/card
